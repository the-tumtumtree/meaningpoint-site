In autumn 2025, the creators of the Israeli cosmetics brand [Dr. Sea](https://doctor-sea.com) came to us. They were developing a new cosmetics brand and were looking for a name.

They had a clear understanding of the audience and many specific wishes for the name. For example, it had to convey a feeling of rest, relaxation, and pleasure. It also needed to be associated with instant anti-stress and simple, pleasant emotions, without overpromising or excessive detail. And, on the contrary, it should not rely on the power of nature or the power of science, because many competitors do that. Most importantly, it had to be possible to register the trademark.

We came up with 13 options. More restrained ones, such as <span class="case-accent">SHORT VACATION</span>. Romantic ones, like <span class="case-accent">SECRET DUNE</span>. Playful ones, like <span class="case-accent">THEY CAN WAIT</span>. But <span class="case-accent">SEA INSIDE</span> was the one that worked. It works both with the packaging — as if you open a jar and find a little bit of the sea inside — and with the mood.

Creating a new brand takes time, but now you can try <span class="case-accent">SEA INSIDE</span>. And we are very happy about that.
