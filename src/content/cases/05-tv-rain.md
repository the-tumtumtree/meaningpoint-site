We're happy to share our collaboration with TV Rain, one of the few independent Russian media outlets, now operating from the Netherlands. Together, we developed a warm and timely New Year campaign to boost recurring donations — a vital source of support for independent journalism in exile.

The mechanics were simple: everyone who set up a recurring donation to TV Rain before the New Year received a signed postcard from one of the channel’s hosts — from Amsterdam with love. When making a donation, supporters could choose who they wanted to receive a personally written New Year’s message from.

The campaign delivered strong results and felt genuinely heartfelt. As TV Rain team puts it: "This time, Meaning Point helped us pull off a small New Year’s miracle, one that will have an impact well beyond this campaign. They came up with the concept, and we brought it to life. The result: the number of recurring donations tripled."
