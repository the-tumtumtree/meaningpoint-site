All tasks are great, but what we love most is working on a project systematically and from different angles. That was Aurora’s story.

Aurora is an AI companion and routine planner designed especially for busy women who need to manage everything at once — work, family, household tasks, self-realisation, social expectations, and all those other wonderful things.

We started with the brand basics — a fairly short document describing the product’s character, essence, and tone of voice. This kind of document is especially useful at the beginning of a new project because it makes it much easier to align on communications. For Aurora, this was particularly important because there were several voices involved — the product team and Aurora herself in conversations with users.

The brand basics became the foundation for everything we did later. We wrote update emails and website copy, worked on the interface, analysed reviews and support messages, and created in-product stories. It was a long and beautiful journey — a full year of ongoing support.
